Usage:
BonBon is a website, specific to Manhattan, that gives you a curated list of fun things to do in the city
based on your specific needs.

The homepage has a list of some random places/events to go to.

When you click "plan your day", you can select any combination of attributes to create a list of things to do in
the city.
